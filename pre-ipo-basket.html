What is a Pre-IPO Basket?

A pre-IPO basket is a carefully curated portfolio of promising private companies that are expected to list on the stock exchange in the future. The goal is to provide investors with early exposure to these companies before they go public, potentially benefiting from price appreciation once the IPO happens.
